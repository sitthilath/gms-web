Call for Abstracts

Submission Deadline

• Call and submission of Abstract deadline: 15th January 2024 to 15th May 2024
• Early registration for participants: 30th April 2024 to 31st May 2024
• Late registration for participants: 1st May 2024 to 15th June 2024
• Selection of abstracts to be presented: 1st June 2024
• Announcement of abstracts to be presented: 5th June 2024

Abstract Guidelines

Abstract submission should be in English: maximum 300 words, Font: Times New Roman, font size of title is 14 (Capital, centered, and bold), font size of contents is 12, including:
• Author (with name of organization)
• Introduction
• Objectives
• Methods
• Results
• Conclusion
• Keywords: The authors must provide 3-6 keywords

All abstracts will be submitted to 14gmsabtract@edu.uhs.la
